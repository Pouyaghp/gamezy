GAMEZY IMAGE GUIDE
============================================================

Drop your images into the folders below using these EXACT names.
Missing files just fall back to coloured placeholders, so add what you like.
Recommended: JPG. Posters ~600x800 (3:4). Category/scene ~1200x1500 / 1600x900.

1) LOGO
------------------------------------------------------------

  public/favicon.svg            (browser tab icon - replace the file)
